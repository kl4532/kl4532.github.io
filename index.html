About

I am self-taugh programist, graduated in a field connected with industry and enviromental protection. Nevertheless I was always close to computers. First time I have tried programing it was for purpose - I have created small windows forms app calculating exhaust composition from combustion. Sounds scary, but formulas for it were pretty easy. Whole computing process was time consuming, hence I made that calculator. And I realy liked it.

I have decided to dive into programming, because solving problems gives me satisfaction. Of course, sometimes I need to struggle, sometimes I get stuck, sometimes there are even tears and pain... more or less. But how they say - no pain, no gain. And in the end of the day - I am proud of my hard work.

In web development i prefer practical approach. I am more interested in logic behind the program than it's design. I like simplicity - more complicated solution -> more bugs.

Why I chose this branch? I want to improve myself, build my knowledgebase day after day, keep my mind busy and never stop learning because there is always room for improvement. And if you stop, you're moving back.
